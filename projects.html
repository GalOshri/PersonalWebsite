Ripple

Free your story
October 2014 - June 2015

A platform for spreading content across the world based on user curation. People share a story or image ("a ripple") and it is sent to nearby users. Each of them chooses whether to dismiss or spread the ripple to more people. Everyone can track how the ripple spreads across the world, and the users love seeing that their post has reached a new continent.

Kefi

Find your night
December 2013 - May 2014

Yelp crowdsources venue reviews, but does not consider that the best-rated club doesn't have a great DJ tonight. Kefi makes it fun to review places and prioritizes recent reviews so people can quickly find venue that matches their mood and expectations.
